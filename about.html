I'm Renée, and I love to write.

When I was six years old, I created my own newspaper, The Bubble News, to inform my family on all elementary school affairs.

I quickly developed an enthusiasm for understanding the joys and challenges of the communities around me. Years later, I've held onto that enthusiasm as I aspire to make technology accessible and engaging for all through words and thoughtful design.

Today, I'm a Technical Writer at Google, designing content to simplify our enterprise advertising products. Before this, I co-oped at IBM Data and AI↗. I also studied Information Science (UX) and English at Cornell University, where I designed community-focused apps for Cornell AppDev↗, wrote for student publications, and admired the Ithaca sunsets.
